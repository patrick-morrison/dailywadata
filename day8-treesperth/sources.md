# Trees in the City

**Source:** [City of Perth - Trees in the City](https://catalogue.data.wa.gov.au/dataset/perth-trees-in-the-city)

**Description:**
This dataset shows point locations of public trees inventoried by the City of Perth. Data is compiled from field capture from our parks team. This is not a complete comprehensive inventory of all trees as trees in the private realm are excluded.
